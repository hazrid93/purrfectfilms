About

From developing stories, scripting, storyboarding to delivering the final product, we strategize on executing the whole project with your objectives in mind, resulting in the "Purrfect" outcome.

With professional film production workflow, our talented young filmmakers work together to achieve the best outcome in our projects. We are equipped with a list of fresh producers, directors, cinematographers, and many more.

Our journey in delivering the "Purrfect" results doesn't end in production, so we work together with you closely to give you the best result during editing. We edit, sound design, and color grade to give the finest cinematic quality in our works.

Our team

irfan nadzree

Director - Cinematographer - Editor

More info

A critical thinker and the ace of spades, Irfan Nadzree is the person when it comes to the technical aspects of all things filmmaking be it from preproduction to post production. He is known for his vast skillset in filmmaking, he has worked on more than 30 productions as Director, Cinematographer, Editor, Colorist and a few more roles.

shima scarlett​​

Producer - Writer - Director​

More info

The philosophical artist, Shima Scarlett has been in the local art scene as both an artist and writer, now puts her talent in film. Being very critical with her works, she brings empathatic stories regarding the human condition on screen. Mainly write and direct, she has done projects ranging from local to international content. She also has had experience working as a casting director in Pinewood Studios for the project Anak Merdeka. 

seyn jukey

Manager - Producer

More info

Coming from the local art scene, Seyn Jukey has experience in dealing with anything that has to do with art. His never ending burn of passion to better the nation’s art appreciation is his main drive in being in this field. In 9 years inside arts industry, he has founded creative agency, publishing house, art space and own creative festival to his records. That is the summed up for almost 500 art events nationality and internationally with more than 20 writers under his management including Fynn Jamal and Takahara Suiko.
